Classic Thai Food Recipes

Thai cuisine is the national cuisine of Thailand. Thai cooking places emphasis on lightly prepared dishes with strong aromatic components and a spicy edge.

Dry Noodles

The super-tasty noodles dish that is quick and easy to make.

  • 85 g - egg noodles
  • 1 tsp - galic oil
  • 1/2 tsp - Molden Mountain sauce
  • 1 tsp - fish sauce
  • 2 tsp - black soy sauce
  • 1 tsp - sugar
  • 1 tsp - white vinegar
