Dry Noodles

The super-tasty noodles dish that is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients:

85 g egg noodles
2 g Bok Cho
1 tsp galic oil
1/2 tsp Molden Mountain sauce
1 tsp fish sauce
2 tsp black soy sauce
1/2 tsp sugar
1 tsp white vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Combine all noodle seasoning ingredients in a mixing bowl, set aside.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, blanch the book choy for 30 seconds. Remove with skimmer and set aside.
  3. Add noodles to the boiling water. Cook about 4 minutes or until cooked. Drain the noodles well and place them directly into the seasoning bowl.
  4. Immediately toss the noodles in the seasoning, be very thorough with the tossign to make sure the sauce is evently absorbed. Place noodles in a serving bowl.
  5. Place Bok Choy at the bottom of the bowl.
  6. Place noodles on top and top with cooked meat of your choice.
  7. top with fried galic, white pepper, chili powder, and chopped cilantro and green onions
  8. Serve immediately, enjoy!
